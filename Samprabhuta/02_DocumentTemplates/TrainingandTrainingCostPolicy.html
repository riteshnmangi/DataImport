FOUND AND MISSING POLICY


We have our own Found and Missing policy designed for all trainees / employees (On-Probation and Permanent)


  1. Scope

    The objective of this policy is to establish clear guidelines for reporting and managing found and missing items within the workplace. The company is committed to maintaining a respectful and responsible environment regarding personal property.

    The company acknowledges that employees may misplace personal belongings during their time on the premises. This policy outlines the procedures for handling found items and reporting missing belongings to ensure that all employees' property is treated with respect and care.


  2. Found Items

    • Reporting Found Items: Employees who find lost items should report them to the designated HR Team. A description of the item, the location where it was found, and the date should be documented.

    • Storing Found Items: Found items will be stored securely by the HR Team until claimed. Items should be labeled with the date found and a description, ensuring that they are organized and easily identifiable.

    • Claiming Found Items: Individuals who believe they have lost an item should contact the HR Team to provide a description of the item. The Coordinator will check the inventory of found items and facilitate the return of the item if it is available.

    • Unclaimed Items: Items that remain unclaimed after 30 days may be donated to charity, disposed of, or repurposed according to company policies. Employees will be notified before any actions are taken regarding unclaimed items.


  3. Missing Items

    • Reporting Missing Items: Employees who discover that their belongings are missing should report the loss to their immediate supervisor and the HR Team as soon as possible. A detailed description of the missing item, including its value, should be provided.

    • Investigation of Missing Items: The Company will conduct a thorough investigation into reported missing items, which may involve checking security footage, interviewing potential witnesses, and reviewing any relevant information.

    • Confidentiality: All reports of missing items will be handled with confidentiality. The company will take reasonable steps to protect the identity of the reporting employee throughout the investigation process.


  4. Company Responsibility

    Company is not responsible for any of the belonging of employee. We expect employees to not bring in valuable ornaments, documents, gazettes or any other instruments. Company is not responsible to take care of their valuables individually. In case of lost or suspected theft, company reserves rights to see CCTV by company official and not by employee in person.


  5. Employee Responsibility

    Employees are responsible for securing their personal belongings and ensuring they are labeled or identifiable. The company encourages employees to avoid leaving valuables unattended in common areas or unsecured locations.
